01 — On friction

The surface is never silent.

Every room keeps a record of contact: a burnished handle, an uneven floorboard, the pale crescent on a favourite cup. The body edits its surroundings one return at a time.

Above: carbon transfer, pressed into stock paper. A mark made by passing through.
